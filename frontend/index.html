Welcome to Uni-Find

We created this platform to help students easily find and report lost items within the university and surrounding college areas. Our goal is to connect people who lose things with those who find them in a simple, fast, and reliable way. Instead of searching blindly or relying on chance, this website makes the process organized and accessible for everyone in the university community, making it easier to return lost belongings to their owners.
